This application allows you to calculate custom duties of vehicle when importing to Ukraine for individuals.

The calculator has two modes. In the first (forward calculation), you can find out the cost of customs clearance of a particular vehicle by it parameters. In the second (backward calculation) you can estimate the maximum purchase price of the vehicle overseas, by defining target cist in Ukraine. All calculations are carried out in euros.

The application is designed for a rough estimate of the costs of customs clearance of the car. Results can be rounded in one direction or another. In general, computational error does not exceed a few euros. Please note that the basic vehicle price is determined by means of catalogs Schwackeliste, so it may differ significantly from the actual purchase price. Also, some commodity codes calculation is simplified to facilitate use of the program.

To determine the exact price of customs clearance, please contact directly to the experts. The results of this application is just an estimate.

The calculation is performed on the basis of the legislation of Ukraine, valid on 20.11.2015 (official documents, Ukrainian):

  • Tax code
  • Customs tariff
  • Law of Ukraine "On measures to stabilize the balance of payments"
  • Note of ICIT about cancel of protection measures on imports into Ukraine cars regardless of country of origin and export

Starting from 01.01.2016, Ukraine cancels 5% additional import toll. This update will be provided with the next version

By 20.11.2015 the Parlament of Ukraine is prestudying of the law project 3251 published at 07.10.2015. This assumes a great decrease for tolls for the used vehicles.
